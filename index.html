Our Story

About Us

Shreeji Bapa Bakery was founded in 2022 by Kinjal Shah with a simple yet profound mission — to bring pure, fresh, and completely egg-free bakery delights to every home.

Every product we create follows the sacred guidelines of The God Swaminarayan, ensuring not just physical purity but also the sanctity of our craft and ingredients.

From celebration cakes to natural ice creams, from cookies to handcrafted chocolates — each item is made with love, care, and an unwavering commitment to quality.
